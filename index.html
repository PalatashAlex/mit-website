About Me

I study at the Uzhhorod Scientific Lyceum in Ukraine. I'm passionate about electronics, physics, programming, and engineering. I'm a winner of several math and physics olympiads. In my free time, I build devices using microcontrollers with Flowcode and Arduino.

Why MIT?

MIT is a place where innovation, research, and technology leadership come together. I want to be among the best, work in advanced laboratories, and join a community that shapes the future of science.

I'm especially inspired by research in artificial intelligence and embedded systems, including the projects at the CSAIL lab.
